## FFmpeg Helper

a FFmpeg-based tool to cut/merge video, extract audio from the video and merge video & audio to new video.

### cut video

mode:
- DURATION_MODE
	- time interval: start_offset ~ start_offset + duration
- ENDTIME_MODE
	- time interval: start_offset ~ end_offset
